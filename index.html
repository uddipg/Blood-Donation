Why you should donate blood?

There are many benefits of blood n and not only you can help other by saving their lives but can also help yourself in living a healthy life.

Maintains Healthy Heart & Liver

Blood donation is beneficial in reducing the risk of heart and liver ailments caused by the iron overload in the body. Intake of iron-rich diet may increase the iron levels in the body.

Learn More

Weight Loss

Regular blood donation reduces the weight of the donors. This is helpful to those who are obese and are at higher risk of cardiovascular diseases and other health disorders.

Learn More

Stimulates Blood Cell Production

After donating blood, the body works to replenish the blood loss. This stimulates the production of new blood cells and in turn, helps in maintaining good health.
